ESG Reporting Hub Industry Examples

Sector-specific scenarios showing how esg reporting hub works across different industries.

Manufacturing

Key ESG topics: emissions (E1), pollution (E2), worker safety (S1), supply chain labor (S2). High materiality on environmental metrics. Governance focus: anti-corruption in procurement, board oversight of climate. Typical frameworks: CSRD/ESRS + GRI. Terrnx approach: integrated EHS and ESG data platform.

Financial Services

Key ESG topics: financed emissions (E1), governance (G1), business conduct. Scope 3 Category 15 is critical and complex. Social: financial inclusion, customer protection. Governance: risk management integration. Typical frameworks: CSRD + TCFD/ISSB + SASB. Terrnx approach: portfolio emissions + climate risk.

Technology

Key ESG topics: energy & data centers (E1), own workforce (S1), consumer data privacy (S4), board diversity (G1). E-waste and responsible mineral sourcing are emerging priorities. Typical frameworks: CSRD + GRI + SASB. Terrnx approach: IT carbon management, DEI analytics, data governance.

Healthcare & Pharma

Key ESG topics: product lifecycle (E5), patient safety (S4), workforce wellbeing (S1), ethical marketing (G1). Access to medicines in developing countries is a social priority. Clinical trial diversity is increasingly scrutinized. Terrnx approach: pharmaceutical supply chain ESG, patient access metrics.

Energy & Utilities

Key ESG topics: climate transition (E1), pollution (E2), biodiversity (E4), water (E3), community relations (S3). Highest ESG scrutiny of any sector. Transition plan credibility is paramount. Terrnx approach: transition planning, biodiversity net gain, just transition analysis.

Consumer Goods

Key ESG topics: packaging & plastics (E5), supply chain labor (S2), product safety (S4), marketing ethics (G1). Deforestation-free supply chains are critical. Circular economy models are emerging. Terrnx approach: supply chain traceability, packaging innovation, circularity metrics.
