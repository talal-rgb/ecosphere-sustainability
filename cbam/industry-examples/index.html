Steel Importers

Iron & steel products (CN codes 7206-7308)

Scenario

A German automotive parts manufacturer imports 2,400 tonnes of steel annually from Turkey and India for stamping and machining operations.

Default Value Calculation

  • • Default emission factor: 2.2 tCO2e/tonne
  • • Annual imports: 2,400 tonnes
  • • Embedded emissions: 5,280 tCO2e
  • • CBAM cost at €75/tonne: €396,000/year

Actual Data Calculation

  • • Turkish supplier (verified): 1.5 tCO2e/tonne
  • • Indian supplier (verified): 1.8 tCO2e/tonne
  • • Weighted average: 1.65 tCO2e/tonne
  • • Embedded emissions: 3,960 tCO2e
  • • CBAM cost at €75/tonne: €297,000/year

Annual Savings: €99,000 (25%)

Verification cost: ~€8,000 | Payback period: 1 month
