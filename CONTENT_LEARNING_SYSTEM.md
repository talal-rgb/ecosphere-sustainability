# Content Learning System

## Objective
Build the highest-traffic and highest-converting sustainability platform in the market.

**Not:** Publishing more articles.  
**Yes:** Learning which content creates traffic and customers.

---

## Core Principles

1. **No content without hypothesis** — Every article must have a predicted outcome
2. **Kill fast, double down faster** — Sunset underperformers, invest in winners
3. **Cluster over keywords** — Build topical authority, not isolated pages
4. **Update > Create** — Improve existing content before creating new
5. **Document everything** — Every decision has a paper trail

---

## System Components

### 1. Article Scorecard
Every article tracked with:
- **Traffic Potential** (1-10): Estimated monthly organic traffic
- **Lead Generation Potential** (1-10): Likelihood to convert visitors
- **Ranking Difficulty** (1-10): Competitive difficulty
- **Business Relevance** (1-10): Alignment with Terrnix offerings
- **Overall Score**: Weighted composite

### 2. Performance Tracking
Per article metrics:
- Publish date, target keyword, search volume
- Ranking position, organic traffic, leads generated
- Conversion rate, engagement metrics

### 3. Lifecycle States
| State | Criteria | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 🚀 Double Down | Score ≥75, Traffic ≥100/mo, Conv ≥5/mo | Expand, promote, cluster |
| ✅ Maintain | Score 50-74, Traffic 20-100/mo | Keep fresh, monitor |
| ⚠️ At Risk | Score 30-49 OR declining 2+ months | Audit, optimize, test |
| 🔧 Consolidate | Score <30, similar content exists | Merge into stronger piece |
| 🌅 Sunset | Score <20, <10/mo for 90+ days | Remove, redirect |

### 4. Monthly Review
Mandatory monthly analysis:
- Highest traffic articles
- Highest converting articles
- Best keyword clusters
- Weak content (sunset candidates)

---

## Feedback Loop

```
Plan → Create → Measure → Learn → Decide → Act
  ↑___________________________________________|
```

1. **Plan**: Hypothesis + scorecard
2. **Create**: Publish with tracking
3. **Measure**: Collect data for 30-60 days
4. **Learn**: Analyze performance
5. **Decide**: Assign lifecycle state
6. **Act**: Optimize, expand, or sunset
