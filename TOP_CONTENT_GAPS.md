## Scoring Methodology

Each gap scored on:
- **Traffic Potential** (1-10): Estimated monthly organic sessions
- **Lead Generation Potential** (1-10): Likelihood to convert to consultation/tool signup
- **Ranking Difficulty** (1-10): Competitive difficulty to rank top 10
- **Business Relevance** (1-10): Alignment with Terrnix services
- **Overall Score** = Traffic×0.25 + Leads×0.30 + (10−Difficulty)×0.20 + Relevance×0.25
