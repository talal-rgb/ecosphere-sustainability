CSRD Industry Examples

Sector-specific scenarios showing how csrd works across different industries.

Manufacturing

Key ESRS topics: E1 Climate (high materiality), E2 Pollution, E3 Water, S1 Own workforce, S2 Workers in value chain. High environmental materiality due to emissions and resource use. Social topics focus on worker safety and supply chain labor. Governance: anti-corruption in procurement. Terrnx approach: integrated EHS data collection, supplier ESG assessments.

Financial Services

Key ESRS topics: E1 Climate (financed emissions — Category 15), G1 Governance, S4 Consumers. Double materiality is complex: banks both finance emitters and face climate transition risk. SBTi guidance for financial institutions is critical. Terrnx approach: portfolio emissions calculation, climate risk stress testing.

Retail

Key ESRS topics: E1 Climate, E5 Resource use, S1 Workforce, S2 Value chain workers, S4 Consumers. Product-level environmental impact is material. Supply chain labor practices are high risk. Consumer data privacy is increasingly important. Terrnx approach: product lifecycle data, supplier audits, consumer impact metrics.

Technology

Key ESRS topics: E1 Climate (data center energy), E5 Resource use (e-waste), S1 Workforce (diversity, wellbeing), S4 Consumers (data privacy). Board diversity and ethical AI are governance priorities. Terrnx approach: IT energy management, responsible sourcing of minerals, DEI metrics.

Energy & Utilities

Key ESRS topics: E1 Climate (transition strategy), E2 Pollution, E3 Water, E4 Biodiversity, G1 Governance. Highest environmental materiality of any sector. Transition plan disclosure is critical. Terrnx approach: scenario analysis, Capex alignment, biodiversity impact assessment.

Healthcare & Pharma

Key ESRS topics: E1 Climate, E2 Pollution, E5 Resource use, S4 Consumers (patient safety), S1 Workforce. Product lifecycle emissions from manufacturing and cold chain. Clinical trial diversity and access to medicines are social priorities. Terrnx approach: pharmaceutical supply chain, patient outcome metrics.
