# SEO Opportunity Scorecard

## Scoring Formula

```
Overall Score = (Traffic Potential × 0.25) + 
                (Lead Generation × 0.30) + 
                ((10 - Ranking Difficulty) × 0.20) + 
                (Business Relevance × 0.25)
```

## Score Components

### Traffic Potential (1-10)
| Score | Monthly Search Volume | Rationale |
|-------|----------------------|-----------|
| 10 | 10,000+ | Massive volume, high impact |
| 8-9 | 5,000-10,000 | Strong volume |
| 6-7 | 1,000-5,000 | Moderate volume |
| 4-5 | 500-1,000 | Niche but valuable |
| 2-3 | 100-500 | Long-tail opportunity |
| 1 | <100 | Low volume |

### Lead Generation Potential (1-10)
| Score | Likelihood | Indicators |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| 10 | Very High | Calculator/tool intent, "template", "software" |
| 8-9 | High | "guide", "how to", "best practices" |
| 6-7 | Moderate | "what is", "vs", "examples" |
| 4-5 | Low | News, informational |
| 1-3 | Very Low | Academic, broad research |

### Ranking Difficulty (1-10)
| Score | Difficulty | Indicators |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| 10 | Extremely Hard | Dominated by major brands, high DA |
| 8-9 | Hard | Strong competitors, established content |
| 6-7 | Moderate | Some competition, winnable |
| 4-5 | Easy | Few competitors, opportunity |
| 1-3 | Very Easy | Blue ocean, quick wins |

### Business Relevance (1-10)
| Score | Alignment | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| 10 | Core offering | Carbon calculator, ESG reporting tools |
| 8-9 | Strong related | Sustainability strategy, compliance |
| 6-7 | Related | Green business, climate tech |
| 4-5 | Tangential | General environmental |
| 1-3 | Weak | Off-topic |

## Batch Scoring Workflow

1. **Research**: Find 10-20 keyword opportunities
2. **Score**: Apply scorecard to each
3. **Rank**: Sort by overall score
4. **Plan**: Top 5 become next month's content
5. **Track**: Add to CONTENT_PERFORMANCE.md

## Rules

- **Never recommend content without scoring**
- **Minimum overall score: 50** to proceed
- **Prioritize scores >70** for immediate action
- **Re-score quarterly** based on actual performance
