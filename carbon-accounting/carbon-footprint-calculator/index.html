Scope 3: Value Chain Emissions

Emissions from your value chain — typically 70-90% of total footprint. GHG Protocol identifies 15 categories. This calculator covers 8 categories.

Category 1: Purchased Goods & Services

GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain Standard, Chapter 5. Typically 20-50% of Scope 3.

Method: Spend-based using EEIO factors (EXIOBASE). Uncertainty: High (±50%).

Item Qty Unit Factor (kg CO₂/unit) Transport km

Method: Activity-based using supplier-specific or LCA database factors. Uncertainty: Low-Medium (±20%).

Formula: Emissions = Σ(Quantity × Unit Factor) + Σ(Transport Distance × Transport Factor)

Reference: GHG Protocol Scope 3 Standard, Chapter 5, p.61 (spend-based), p.65 (supplier-specific)

Category 2: Capital Goods

GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain Standard, Chapter 6. Typically 5-15% of Scope 3. Exclude from Cat 1 to avoid double counting.

Method: Spend-based using capital goods EEIO factors. Amortized over asset lifetime.

Method: Activity-based using lifecycle assessment factors. Annualized = Total / Lifetime.

Formula: Annual Emissions = Σ(Asset Lifecycle Emissions / Lifetime)

Reference: GHG Protocol Scope 3 Standard, Chapter 6. Warning: Do not include in Cat 1.

Category 4: Upstream Transportation & Distribution

GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain Standard, Chapter 8. GLEC Framework / ISO 14083 aligned. Typically 5-15% of Scope 3.

Method: Weight-distance using GLEC Framework factors. Uncertainty: Medium (±30%).

Origin Destination Weight (kg) Mode Distance (km) Reefer

Method: Shipment-level calculation. Reefer adds ~15% to factor. Uncertainty: Low-Medium (±20%).

Formula: Emissions = Σ(Weight (t) × Distance (km) × Mode Factor × Reefer Multiplier)

Reference: GHG Protocol Scope 3 Standard, Chapter 8. GLEC Framework v3.0. ISO 14083:2023.

Category 5: Waste Generated in Operations

GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain Standard, Chapter 9. EPA WARM model. Typically 1-3% of Scope 3.

Method: Waste quantity × disposal method factor. Recycling shows as negative (credit). Uncertainty: Medium (±30%).

Waste Type Quantity (kg) Disposal Method

Method: Waste-type-specific factors from EPA WARM. Uncertainty: Low-Medium (±25%).

Formula: Emissions = Σ(Waste Quantity × Disposal Method Factor)

Reference: GHG Protocol Scope 3 Standard, Chapter 9. EPA WARM v16. DEFRA 2024 Waste Factors.

Category 6: Business Travel

GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain Standard, Chapter 10. DEFRA 2024 factors. Typically 2-10% of Scope 3.

Method: Distance-based using DEFRA 2024 factors. Uncertainty: Low-Medium (±25%).

Method: Flight-specific with class multiplier and radiative forcing. Uncertainty: Medium (±30%).

Formula: Emissions = Distance × Mode Factor × Class Multiplier × Radiative Forcing (if applicable)

Reference: GHG Protocol Scope 3 Standard, Chapter 10. DEFRA 2024 Business Travel Factors. ICAO methodology.

Category 7: Employee Commuting

GHG Protocol Corporate Value Chain Standard, Chapter 11. Typically 2-5% of Scope 3. Growing priority with remote work.

Method: Employee count × distance × days × mode mix. Uncertainty: High (±40%). Survey recommended for accuracy.

Method: Per-employee calculation. Upload CSV for bulk entry. Uncertainty: Low-Medium (±20%).

Formula: Emissions = Σ(Employees × Distance × 2 × Days × Mode Factor)

Reference: GHG Protocol Scope 3 Standard, Chapter 11. DEFRA 2024 Commuting Factors. Survey method recommended.
