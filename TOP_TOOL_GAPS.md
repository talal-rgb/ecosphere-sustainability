## Tool Strategy Principles

1. **Free tools drive traffic** — gate the advanced features or detailed reports behind email
2. **Tools must be genuinely useful** — not marketing gimmicks
3. **Every tool links to consultation CTA** — capture high-intent users
4. **Tools get backlinks** — unique calculators are highly linkable
5. **Tools improve E-E-A-T** — signals expertise and authority to Google
