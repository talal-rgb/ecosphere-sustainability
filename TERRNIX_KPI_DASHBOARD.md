## 🔄 How to Update

1. **Traffic Data**: Export from Google Analytics 4
   - Acquisition > Traffic Acquisition
   - Filter by "Organic Search"
   - Date range: Last 7 days

2. **SEO Data**: Export from Google Search Console
   - Performance tab
   - Date range: Last 7 days
   - Export queries (top 20)

3. **Product Data**: Export from event tracking
   - Calculator events: `calculator_start`, `calculator_complete`
   - PDF events: `pdf_download`
   - Quiz events: `quiz_complete`

4. **Business Data**: Export from form submissions
   - Contact form database
   - Newsletter signup list
   - Lead qualification spreadsheet

5. **Content Data**: Reference CONTENT_PERFORMANCE.md
   - Articles published/updated this week
   - Content scores and states
