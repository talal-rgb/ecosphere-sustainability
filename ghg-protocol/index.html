Who Should Care?

Sustainability Teams

You own the data, the narrative, and the roadmap. The GHG Protocol is the global standard your reports will be judged against.

CFOs & Finance

Carbon is becoming a balance-sheet item. Emissions affect cost of capital, insurance premiums, and green bond eligibility.

Supply Chain Leaders

Scope 3 is where most of your footprint hides. Supplier engagement and data collection are now due-diligence requirements.

Why Now?

Regulatory Deadlines Are Real

CSRD, SEC climate disclosure rules, and CBAM are moving from voluntary to mandatory. Early reporters gain a first-mover advantage.

Investors Are Pricing Climate Risk

Institutional investors now use emissions data in ESG scoring. Poor disclosure can limit access to capital.

Business Risk

  • Penalties: Fines for inaccurate or late reporting under CSRD and national laws.
  • Reputational damage: Greenwashing accusations can destroy brand trust.
  • Supply chain disruption: Suppliers who fail to disclose may be dropped from procurement lists.
  • Exclusion from RFPs: Large buyers now require GHG data from vendors.

Financial Impact

Cost of Carbon

Internal carbon pricing and upcoming border taxes (CBAM) directly hit margins. Measurement is the first step to management.

Green Financing

Better disclosure unlocks sustainability-linked loans and green bonds at lower interest rates.

Operational Efficiency

Carbon hotspots often overlap with cost hotspots. Reducing emissions frequently reduces energy and waste spend.
