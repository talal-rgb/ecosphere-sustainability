Key Standards & Methods

GHG Protocol

The world's most widely used GHG accounting standard. Corporate Standard for organizations, Product Standard for products. Referenced by CSRD, CDP, and SBTi.

ISO 14064

International standard for GHG quantification and verification. Part 1 covers organizational inventories. Often required for carbon neutrality claims.

ISO 14067

Standard for product carbon footprint quantification and communication. Required for product-level environmental claims under EU Green Claims Directive.

PAS 2050

British Standard for product lifecycle GHG emissions. Widely used in UK and Commonwealth markets. Being superseded by ISO 14067 in many jurisdictions.
