About Terrnix

AI-powered sustainability intelligence for carbon accounting, ESG reporting, and climate compliance. Built by experts, for experts.

Our Mission

Terrnix exists to make sustainability intelligence accessible, accurate, and actionable. We combine cutting-edge AI with deep domain expertise in carbon accounting, ESG frameworks, and climate regulations to help organizations make informed decisions.

From Scope 1-3 emissions tracking to CSRD compliance and green finance analysis, Terrnix provides the tools and insights sustainability professionals need to drive real impact.
