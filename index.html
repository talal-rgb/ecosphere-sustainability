GHG Protocol: Scopes 1, 2, 3

Scope 1

Direct Emissions

Sources owned or controlled by your organization.

• Company vehicles
• Manufacturing processes
• On-site fuel combustion
• Fugitive emissions
Scope 2

Indirect (Energy)

Purchased electricity, steam, heating, cooling.

• Purchased electricity
• District heating/cooling
• Steam for processes
• Power from grid
Scope 3

Value Chain

All other indirect emissions. Usually 70-90% of total.

• Purchased goods & services
• Business travel
• Employee commuting
• Use of sold products
