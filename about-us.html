About Terrnix

Intelligence for a Greener Tomorrow

Terrnix is an AI-powered sustainability intelligence platform that combines advanced analytics with deep domain expertise in carbon accounting, ESG frameworks, climate regulations, green finance, and energy economics. We help organizations, investors, and sustainability professionals make data-driven decisions that accelerate the transition to a low-carbon economy.

Founded by a team of climate scientists, policy analysts, and technology engineers, Terrnix was built to address a critical gap in the sustainability landscape: the need for actionable, real-time intelligence that cuts through complexity and delivers clarity. Too many organizations struggle with fragmented data, conflicting standards, and rapidly evolving regulations. Terrnix exists to solve that problem.

Our Mission

We believe that better information leads to better decisions, and better decisions lead to a sustainable future. Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality sustainability intelligence—making the tools, data, and insights that were once available only to the largest organizations accessible to businesses, governments, and individuals worldwide.

What We Do

  • Carbon Accounting: Track, measure, and report greenhouse gas emissions across Scope 1, 2, and 3 using the latest IPCC emission factors and GHG Protocol standards.
  • Renewable Energy Project Design: Design and optimize solar, wind, and hybrid renewable energy projects from concept through to execution, including feasibility studies, grid integration, and financial modelling.
  • Decarbonisation Strategies: Develop comprehensive decarbonisation roadmaps tailored to industry-specific challenges, covering technology selection, investment planning, and transition risk assessment.
  • Decarbonisation Roadmaps: Help organisations build actionable net-zero and decarbonisation roadmaps with clear milestones, KPIs, and governance frameworks aligned to SBTi and national climate targets.
  • Sustainability Regulation Insight: Navigate complex regulatory landscapes including CBAM, SBTi, CSRD, CS3D (CSDDD), ISSB S1/S2, SEC Climate Rules, and the EU Taxonomy—with practical guidance on compliance timelines and reporting obligations.
  • ESG Intelligence: Navigate complex ESG reporting requirements including CSRD, ISSB S1/S2, SEC Climate Rules, and the EU Taxonomy.
  • Climate Policy: Stay ahead of regulations like CBAM, carbon pricing mechanisms, and national net-zero commitments.
  • Energy Economics: Analyze LCOE, NPV, and IRR for renewable energy investments with up-to-date market data.
  • Green Finance: Access intelligence on green bonds, sustainability-linked loans, carbon markets, and transition finance.

Our Team

Terrnix brings together experts from climate science, energy policy, financial analysis, software engineering, chemical engineering, and decarbonisation strategy. Our team includes chemical engineering specialists with deep process industry expertise and decarbonisation strategy experts who have designed net-zero roadmaps for heavy industry across Europe, Asia, and North America. We combine technical engineering rigor with practical implementation experience to deliver solutions that work on the ground.

Our Approach

We take an evidence-based, technology-enabled approach to sustainability intelligence. Every data point on our platform is traceable to its source—whether it's an IPCC emission factor, an IEA market projection, or a regulatory filing. We don't rely on black-box algorithms; we explain our methodologies clearly and update our assumptions as science and markets evolve.
