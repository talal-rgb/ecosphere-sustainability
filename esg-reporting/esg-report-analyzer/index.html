ESG Report Analyzer

Upload your sustainability or ESG report and receive an AI-powered gap analysis against CSRD, ISSB, TCFD, and GRI frameworks. Identify missing disclosures, benchmark against peers, and get actionable recommendations.

Read CSRD Guide

How It Works

1. Upload

Upload your PDF, Word, or HTML sustainability report.

2. Select Framework

Choose CSRD, ISSB, TCFD, GRI, or a custom framework.

3. AI Analysis

Our AI engine scans for gaps, inconsistencies, and missing disclosures.

4. Get Report

Download a detailed gap analysis with actionable recommendations.

Frameworks Supported

CSRD

Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive — EU mandatory disclosure requirements.

ISSB

IFRS S1 & S2 — global baseline for sustainability-related financial disclosures.

TCFD

Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures — governance, strategy, risk, metrics.

GRI

Global Reporting Initiative — comprehensive sustainability impact reporting standards.

Key Features

Gap Detection

Automatically identifies missing disclosures and incomplete sections.

Benchmarking

Compare your disclosures against industry peers and best practices.

Actionable Insights

Get specific recommendations to improve your ESG reporting quality.

Secure & Private

PDF Export

Download your analysis as a branded PDF report for stakeholders.

Track Progress
