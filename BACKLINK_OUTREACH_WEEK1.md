intelligence platform focused on carbon accounting and ESG reporting.

We've just published analysis showing that Morocco alone could capture 
$8 billion in EU carbon market integration by 2030 — if exporters act

I'd like to propose a guest article: "How African Exporters Can Turn 
CBAM Compliance into Competitive Advantage" — with specific data on:

Would this be of interest to your readers?

Best,
Tallal
Founder, Terrnix
