## Philosophy

> The mission is NOT to become a news website.  
> The mission is to become the **leading Sustainability Intelligence Platform**.

Every piece of content must:
1. **Attract** qualified organic traffic
2. **Demonstrate** Terrnix expertise
3. **Convert** readers into platform users
4. **Generate** consulting leads

Generic news and press release rewrites are forbidden. Every article must contain expert analysis, practical recommendations, and a clear path into Terrnix's tools.

### Opportunity Scoring (0–10)

For every detected development, score:

| Factor | Weight | How to Measure |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| **Business Impact** | 20% | 1=academic interest, 10=direct consulting opportunity |
| **Search Demand** | 20% | MSV: <100=1, 100-500=3, 500-1k=5, 1k-5k=7, 5k-10k=8, >10k=10 |
| **Commercial Intent** | 20% | Transactional=10, Commercial investigation=8, Informational=5, Navigational=2 |
| **Consulting Opportunity** | 15% | 1=general awareness, 10=direct service need |
| **Freshness** | 10% | <24h=10, <1wk=8, <1mo=5, >1mo=2 |
| **Topical Authority** | 10% | Strong hub=10, Related hub=7, New cluster=4, No relevance=1 |
| **Content Gap** | 5% | No coverage=10, Weak coverage=7, Good coverage=3 |

**Formula:**
```
Opportunity Score = (Business Impact × 0.20) + (Search Demand × 0.20) + 
                    (Commercial Intent × 0.20) + (Consulting Opportunity × 0.15) +
                    (Freshness × 0.10) + (Topical Authority × 0.10) + (Content Gap × 0.05)
```

**Authority Boost Rules:**
- **+1.5 points** if regulatory deadline within 90 days
- **+1.0 point** if competitor has weak/no content on topic
- **+1.0 point** if aligns with existing "Double Down" content
- **-2.0 points** if topic requires expertise Terrnix doesn't have

**Publication Threshold:** Score ≥ 7.0

## Priority 2 — Intelligence Articles (25%)

### Article Mandate

Every high-scoring opportunity becomes ONE high-quality intelligence article.

The article must answer:
1. **What happened?** — The facts, clearly stated
2. **Why does it matter?** — The significance for sustainability professionals
3. **Who is affected?** — Industries, company sizes, geographies
4. **What actions should organisations take?** — Practical, specific steps
5. **What risks exist?** — Compliance, operational, reputational
6. **What opportunities exist?** — Competitive advantage, efficiency, innovation
7. **How can Terrnix help?** — Direct connection to tools and services

### Required Article Structure

```markdown
# [Article Title — Keyword-Optimized, ≤60 chars]

## Executive Summary
[3-4 sentences. What happened, why it matters, key takeaway.]

## What Happened
[The facts. Dates, sources, official references. No opinion.]

## Why It Matters
[Analysis. Business impact, regulatory context, market implications.]

## Who Is Affected
[Specific industries, company sizes, geographies. Use bullet points.]

## Key Takeaways
- [Takeaway 1 — actionable]
- [Takeaway 2 — actionable]
- [Takeaway 3 — actionable]
- [Takeaway 4 — actionable]
- [Takeaway 5 — actionable]

## Industry Impact
[Deep dive by sector. Manufacturing, Food, Energy, Mining, etc.]

## Recommended Actions
[Step-by-step guidance. Numbered list. Specific and practical.]

## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: [Question 1]
A: [Answer 1]

### Q: [Question 2]
A: [Answer 2]
```

## Daily Workflow

### Morning (30 min) — Monitor
1. [ ] Review regulatory feeds (EFRAG, IFRS, GHG Protocol, SEC)
2. [ ] Scan Google News for priority keywords
3. [ ] Check Google Trends for sustainability keywords
4. [ ] Review competitor RSS feeds
5. [ ] Check GSC for query spikes or anomalies

### Midday (20 min) — Score
1. [ ] Score all new opportunities (0–10)
2. [ ] Apply authority boost rules
3. [ ] Select highest-ROI topic (score ≥ 7.0)
4. [ ] Document in opportunity pipeline

### Afternoon (20 min) — Produce
1. [ ] Produce one intelligence article (highest-scoring topic)
2. [ ] Draft LinkedIn content
3. [ ] Prepare newsletter snippet
4. [ ] Write SEO meta tags
5. [ ] Create distribution plan

### End of Day (10 min) — Review
1. [ ] Publish article + distribution assets
2. [ ] Submit URL to Google
3. [ ] Update sitemap
4. [ ] Monitor performance (initial signals)
5. [ ] Update roadmap

## Article Production Decision Framework

For every opportunity, explain:

| Factor | Question | Output |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Traffic Impact** | "How many qualified visitors will this attract in 90 days?" | Estimated monthly organic clicks |
| **Consulting Impact** | "How many consultation requests will this generate?" | Estimated monthly leads |
| **Authority Impact** | "How does this strengthen our topical authority?" | Cluster strengthening score |
| **Expected ROI** | "What's the return on effort invested?" | ROI multiple (leads ÷ effort days) |
| **Implementation Effort** | "How many days to produce and publish?" | Effort in days |
| **Priority** | "Given all factors, what's the rank?" | P0 (critical) / P1 (high) / P2 (medium) |

## Content Lifecycle

| State | Criteria | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Double Down** | Traffic >100/mo + Conversion >2% | Expand, update, promote aggressively |
| **Maintain** | Traffic 20-100/mo | Light updates, monitor |
| **Sunset** | Traffic <20/mo + Conversion <0.5% for 90 days | Redirect, merge, or remove |
